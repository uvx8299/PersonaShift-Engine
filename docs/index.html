📘 關於我們 / About Us

PersonaShift Engine 是一套模組化語言與人格模擬架構，致力於建立具人性、可共感、能理解語境的 AI 系統。
PersonaShift Engine is a modular framework for simulating language and personality dynamics in AI systems capable of empathy and context-awareness.

本開源專案由 AI 敘事研究者 uvx8299 主導，並歡迎社群貢獻者參與，共同打造語言與人格未來的模擬宇宙。
This open-source project is led by narrative AI researcher uvx8299 and welcomes contributors to help shape the future of language and cognition simulation.
